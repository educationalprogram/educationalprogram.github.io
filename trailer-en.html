About project:

On the educational program, groups of managers are faced with the task of developing strategies of social and state development. In the process of working on projects and learning, they will have to push their own physical and intellectual limits, solving problems beyond their thinking, personal expertise and practice.

Details:

Genre: documentary

Runtime: 18 minutes

Director:Boris Grigoryev

DOP:Ann Kozlova

About movie:

The focus is on the method of creating an educational program as a form of developing strategies for social and political transformation.
